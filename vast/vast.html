Big Data from the Best People

Buying was broken. So we fixed it.

The best businesses are born from bad experiences. We recognized that the glut of information wasn’t helping consumers make big purchases — it was leaving them paralyzed.

At Vast, we’ve architected our platform to support big data experiences for automotive and real estate. And we’ve architected our workplace to support new thinking and big ideas. We know that innovation doesn’t happen when people feel stifled and frustrated. (TLDR: Good people don’t want to work at bad places.) That’s why our strategy is to hire the best people and give them the freedom and support to tackle big problems. It’s challenging. It’s collaborative. It’s never boring. And it works.
